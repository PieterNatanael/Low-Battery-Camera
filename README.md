# Low-Battery-Camera

Privacy Policy

LB Camera — Low Battery Camera
Developed by Pieter Yoshua Natanael
Last updated: April 2026

---

1. Overview

LB Camera does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. 
We have no servers, no user accounts, and no analytics. Everything the 
app does happens entirely on your device.

---

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or crash reports sent to us
- Device identifiers
- Any recordings, photos, or videos you capture

---

3. Camera and Microphone

LB Camera requests access to your device's camera and microphone solely 
to record video and capture photos within the app. This data never leaves 
your device unless you explicitly choose to save or share it.

---

4. Photo Library

LB Camera requests write-only access to your photo library when you choose 
to save a recording. We do not read the contents of your photo library.

---

5. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We 
do not see, store, or handle your payment details.

To manage or cancel your subscription:
**Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → LB Camera**

Subscription terms:
- Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.
- Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours 
  before the end of the current billing period.
- Your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end 
  of the current period.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

---

6. Local Storage

The app stores the following locally on your device only:

- Your selected video quality setting
- A subscription expiry timestamp (to avoid unnecessary network calls)
- Whether you have accepted the in-app disclaimer

None of this data is transmitted anywhere.

---

7. Third-Party Services

LB Camera does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, 
analytics platforms, or tracking tools.

---

8. Children's Privacy

LB Camera does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including 
children under 13. No personal information is ever collected.

---

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with 
an updated date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes 
constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
